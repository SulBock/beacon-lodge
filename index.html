Beacon Lodge

Beacon lodge is a place of comfort and relaxation for travelers, complete with free WiFi throughout the property. This hotel offers guests flat-screen TVs with satellite channels, slippers, free toiletries, and private parking.


All of the rooms include a private bathroom and come equipped with convenient kettles for added convenience, and for those travelling from freetown to the Southern is a very beautiful place where you and your family can relax and have a good time, great parking space and an out-door to relax under the palm trees after a long stressful day.


Beacon Lodge is situated at 30 Rashidu Street, Lewabu Bo, Southern Province of Sierra Leone.
